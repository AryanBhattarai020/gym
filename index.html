About Richard Fitness

Your premier destination for fitness excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by fitness enthusiast Richard Thompson, Richard Fitness has grown from a small local gym to the city's premier fitness destination. Our mission is simple: to provide world-class facilities and expert guidance to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Our Mission

We believe fitness is not just about physical strength, but about building confidence, community, and a healthier lifestyle. Every member of our gym family receives personalized attention and support on their unique fitness journey.
